Current datasets on TSA-ML platform

Taiwan Social Change Survey (TSCS) is a longitutindal dataset containing survey data on different social topics such as employment, family, and social networks. The TSCS contains data from the 1980's from individuals and families across Taiwan. This asset is managed by the Center for Survey Research, Academia Sinica, Taiwan. More detail information is available here.

World Values Survey (WVS) has a collection of survey datasets of social and economic indicators of individuals. It contains deidentified data from the 1980s of different countries including those of East-Asia, America, and Europe. The TSA-ML platform currently contains data from Hong Kong, Macao, Taiwan, and China. A network of researchers from different countries have rigiously sampled the population and collected representative information of individuals and families. TSA-ML

An AI-based platform that integrates consumer data from diverse sources

TSA-ML is a sophisticated data science platform for consumer market research


Our platform extract, transform, clean, and link consumer data from diverse campaigns and sources into a single Knowledge Graph using W3C standards. The platform is ideal in connecting your organisation's customer data together and to perform advance market research analytics, machine learning, and AI.
